ABOUT NAKSHA

NAKSHASTUDIO is an established designing based firm in DELHI. We provide specialized assistance in commercial, residential, hospitality, and institutional projects and also venture executional and project management services.

Our goal is to provide maximum comfort and visual appeal for our client, conceptualizing parallelly with their demands. Here in NAKSHASTUDIO our team extensively takes every project through a creative process performing various discussions, studies, research and applications. We believe the back and forth process of any design project makes it more refined and efficient.

Every project has its own needs and requirements and it's our job to provide our clients their surreal utopia.
